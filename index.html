U-MEKING STORY

Crafting Quality Since 1999

Premium Garment Accessories & Promotional Products

U-Meking Inc started out as a family-operated custom embroidery business in 1999. Since 2008, we have evolved into a comprehensive producer and distributor of garment accessories and printing package products. Over 19 years of dedication, U-Meking has become one of the nation's leading suppliers, having delivered over 70 million products to more than 3,500 clothing brands worldwide.

Your trust makes us progress all the time. As a designated accessories manufacturer for numerous listed clothing enterprises, we combine 19 years of professional experience with ISO9001, SGS, and Oeko-Tex Standard 100 certifications. We guarantee factory-direct pricing with no hidden fees, ensuring exceptional quality and value for every partnership.
